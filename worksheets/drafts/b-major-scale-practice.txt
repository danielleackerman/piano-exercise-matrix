VARIATIONS
================================================================

    Contrary Motion
    Any scale or arpeggio exercise can be played in contrary
    motion ‚Äî one hand ascending while the other descends
    simultaneously. This is harder than parallel motion and
    excellent brain training.

    Accent & Rhythmic Variations
    Apply to any scale or arpeggio exercise for fluidity and
    evenness:
    a  Accent every note ‚Äî extra weight on each individual note.
    b  Accent every second note ‚Äî grouped, rolling feel.
    c  Triplet groupings ‚Äî organize the scale in groups of three.
    d  Groups of four ‚Äî organize the scale in groups of four.
    e  Prolong one note per group ‚Äî hold the 2nd or 3rd note
       slightly longer to build control.

    Staccato
    Any scale exercise can be played staccato ‚Äî lift from each
    key rather than connecting the notes. Treat it as a separate
    pass, not a replacement for legato practice.
